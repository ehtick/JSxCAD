### outline()
Produces segments representing the geometric edges of the shape.

See: [edges](#https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jsxcad/JSxCAD/master/nb/api/edges.nb)

```JavaScript
Arc(3, 3, 1)
  .outline()
  .view()
  .note('Arc(3, 3, 1).outline() shows the geometric shape.');
```
